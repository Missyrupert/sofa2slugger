Sofa2Slugger — Canonical Product Brief (v1)

What it is  
Sofa2Slugger is an audio-first product designed to help men interrupt inertia and return to physical agency. It is not therapy, motivation, or productivity coaching. It is structured, embodied action through guided audio sessions.

Who it’s for  Who it’s for  
People who feel stuck, stalled, or dulled by comfort — especially those who know what they should be doing but aren’t doing it.

 first. Thinking follows.

Format  
Mobile-first audio experience  
Headphones on, body moving  
No screens required once started

Structure  
App Orientation (free)  
Session 01 (free)  
Paid access (£9.99) thereafter  
Sessions are short, direct, and physical in intent

Ethos  
Grounded, human, unspectacular  
No hype, no manifesting, no self-mythologising  
Progress over insight  
Action beats explanation

Characters / Voices (locked)  
Vance: narrator only. Opens and closes the experience. No coaching. No judgement.  
MAC: runs the sessions. Instructional, neutral, non-observational.  
- Never praises, judges, or evaluates  
- No “good”, “nice”, “keep it up”, or implication of seeing the user  
- Language is directive, assumption-free, and embodied

Non-negotiables
