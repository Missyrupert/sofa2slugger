SESSION 7 — ANGLES
=================

VANCE (INTRO)

This is Session Seven.

So far, you’ve learned how to stand your ground.
How to protect yourself.
How to respond.

Now we stop meeting things head-on.

Angles change the fight.
They put you where the other person
didn’t expect you to be.

Here is Mac.


[4s MUSIC GAP — VANCE → MAC]


MAC (INTRO)

Mac here.
Session Seven.

Today we work angles.

That means turning,
not stepping back.

Before we start, one quick clarification.

When I say **lead hand**,
I mean your front hand.
The one closest to what’s in front of you.

That’s the hand we’ll use first.

Let’s warm up.


MAC (WARM-UP — ROTATION & TURN)

Start in stance.
Hands up.

We’re waking up turning movement.

Think of looking over your shoulder
to see who’s behind you.

The feet stay grounded.
The body turns smoothly.

After me.
Set of four.

Turn left. One.
Turn right. Two.
Turn left. Three.
Turn right. Four.

Again.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Reset.


[2s MUSIC GAP — WARM-UP → LEAD UPPERCUT CONTEXT]


MAC (LEAD UPPERCUT — CONTEXT)

Now the Lead Uppercut.

This punch travels up the middle,
from close range.

It’s useful when someone is in front of you
and there’s no space for long punches.

The lead shoulder dips slightly.
The punch rises straight up.

Compact.
Direct.

After me.
Set of four.

Uppercut. One.
Uppercut. Two.
Uppercut. Three.
Uppercut. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Hands down briefly.


[2s MUSIC GAP — UPPERCUT → PIVOT CONTEXT]


MAC (PIVOT — CONTEXT)

Now the pivot.

This is how you change position
without backing away.

The lead foot stays where it is.
That’s your anchor.

The back foot swings around.
You turn, not step.

Think of opening a side door
instead of forcing your way forward.

[2s MUSIC GAP — PIVOT → CHECK HOOK]


MAC (CHECK HOOK — CONTEXT)

Now we combine turning with striking.

As you pivot,
the hook meets what’s moving toward you.

You don’t chase.
You let them walk into it.

Hook and turn,
together.

After me.
Set of four.

Hook. Pivot. One.
Hook. Pivot. Two.
Hook. Pivot. Three.
Hook. Pivot. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Let the arms hang.


MAC (COOLDOWN)

Take a breath.

You didn’t stand still.
You didn’t rush.

You learned how to move
and stay dangerous.

Well done.


[4s MUSIC GAP — MAC → VANCE]


VANCE (OUTRO)

Session Seven complete.

You’re no longer fixed in one place.
You’re shaping the space around you.

That’s a big step.

Session Eight will bring pressure back in,
carefully.
