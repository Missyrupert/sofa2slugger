SESSION 5 — DEFENCE & AWARENESS
==============================

VANCE (INTRO)

This is Session Five.

So far, you’ve learned how to move
and how to generate force.

Now we focus on something just as important.

Staying safe while you do it.

Defence isn’t retreat.
It’s intelligence.

Here is Mac.


[4s MUSIC GAP — VANCE → MAC]


MAC (INTRO)

Mac here.
Session Five.

Today is defence.

Not panic.
Not covering up.

Small movements.
Awareness.
Staying present.

If any of this feels unfamiliar,
that’s fine.
We keep it simple.


MAC (WARM-UP — HEAD MOVEMENT)

Start in stance.
Hands up.

We’re waking up the head and knees.

The body stays tall.
The head moves just enough.

Set of four.

Slip right. Two.
Slip left. Three.
Slip right. Four.

Again.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.

Four.

And stop. Stay loose.


[2s MUSIC GAP — WARM-UP → SLIP CONTEXT]


MAC (SLIP — CONTEXT)

When you slip,
you’re not ducking.

You’re letting the punch miss.

Small bend in the knees.
Head moves off the centre line.
Eyes forward.

You stay balanced.
You stay ready.

Last set.

[2s MUSIC GAP — SLIP → ROLL CONTEXT]


MAC (ROLL — CONTEXT)

Now the roll.

This deals with hooks.

You’re not folding.
You’re changing level.

Knees bend.
Hips turn.
Head passes underneath.

Think smooth,
not deep.


MAC (ROLL PRACTICE)

[2s MUSIC GAP — ROLL → DEFENCE INTO OFFENCE]

Defence matters
because it puts you in position.

Slip.
Cross.

Nothing forced.
Just available.

After me.
Set of four.

MAC (COOLDOWN)

If this felt awkward at times,
that’s normal.

Awareness takes practice.

You stayed with it.

We’re done.


[4s MUSIC GAP — MAC → VANCE]

Session Five complete.

You’re not just throwing punches now.

Session Six builds from here.
Rest up.
