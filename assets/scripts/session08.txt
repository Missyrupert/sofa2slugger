=====================
SESSION 8 — BODY WORK
=====================

VANCE (INTRO)

This is Session Eight.

Everyone headhunts.
It’s instinct.
But the body is a bigger target.
And it doesn't move as fast.

Body shots drain the fuel tank.
They slow the opponent down.
They bring the hands down.

Today we go downstairs.

Mac.

Mac here.
Session Eight.
Body shots.

We don't punch down with our arms.
We change levels with our legs.
Get to their height.

We’ll work the Jab to the body.
And the Cross to the body.

Warm up.
Squats.
Bodyweight.
Keep the back straight.
Get the legs ready for level changing.

Sixty seconds.
I'm stepping out.

MAC (JAB TO BODY)

Stance.

Jab to Body.

Step in deeper than usual.
Bend the knees.
Drive the jab into the solar plexus.

Keep your head safe.
Other hand high.

Jab Body. Reset.

Forty-five seconds.
I'm watching.

[WORKSPACE — 45s]

MAC (CROSS TO BODY)

Cross to Body.

Same thing.
Step across.
Drop level.
Drive the cross into the stomach.

It’s a commitment.
You’re vulnerable if you miss.
So don't miss.

Cross Body. Reset.

Forty-five seconds.
Start now.

[WORKSPACE — 45s]
(silence)
