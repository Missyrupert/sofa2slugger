SESSION 3 — THE LEAD HOOK
=======================

VANCE (INTRO)

This is Session Three.

You’ve learned how to stand.
You’ve learned how to reach.

Now we add something different.

Straight punches move through space.
Hooks move around it.

They change the conversation.

Here is Mac.


[4s MUSIC GAP — VANCE → MAC]


MAC (INTRO)

Mac here.
Session Three.

Today we add the Lead Hook.

This punch doesn’t travel far.
It travels sideways.

It’s about rotation.
About timing.
About where you place yourself.

We start by waking the body up.


MAC (WARM-UP — ROTATION)

Feet under you.
Hands up.

We’re loosening the torso.

Think of your body like a lighthouse.
The base stays solid.
The top rotates smoothly.

After me.
Set of four.

Rotate left. One.
Rotate right. Two.
Rotate left. Three.
Rotate right. Four.

Again.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Back to stance.


[2s MUSIC GAP — WARM-UP → STRAIGHTS REVIEW]


MAC (STRAIGHTS REVIEW — CONTEXT)

Quick reminder.

The jab sets the line.
The cross travels through it.

These punches keep you honest.
They stop you reaching.

After me.
Set of four.

Jab. Cross. One.
Jab. Cross. Two.
Jab. Cross. Three.
Jab. Cross. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Hands down briefly.


[2s MUSIC GAP — STRAIGHTS → LEAD HOOK CONTEXT]


MAC (LEAD HOOK — CONTEXT)

Now the Lead Hook.

This punch is close.
It’s compact.

Think of throwing your elbow
around the edge of a table.

You don’t reach.
You turn.

Front foot pivots.
Front hip follows.
The arm comes along for the ride.

The power comes from rotation,
not from swinging.

We take it slow.


MAC (LEAD HOOK PRACTICE)

After me.
Set of four.

Hook. One.
Hook. Two.
Hook. Three.
Hook. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Drop the hands.


[2s MUSIC GAP — HOOK → COMBINATION]


MAC (COMBINATION — CONTEXT)

Now we connect it.

Jab.
Cross.
Hook.

Straight lines first.
Then around the corner.

After me.
Set of four.

Jab. Cross. Hook. One.
Jab. Cross. Hook. Two.
Jab. Cross. Hook. Three.
Jab. Cross. Hook. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Let the arms hang.


MAC (COOLDOWN)

Take a breath.

Rotation uses different muscles.
That’s normal.

You’re learning how to turn,
not force.

We’re done.


[4s MUSIC GAP — MAC → VANCE]


VANCE (OUTRO)

Session Three complete.

You’ve added a new shape.
A new angle.

This is where boxing starts to feel
less linear.

Session Four is waiting.
