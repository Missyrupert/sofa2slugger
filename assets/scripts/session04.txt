SESSION 4 — REAR HAND POWER
==========================

VANCE (INTRO)

This is Session Four.

So far, you’ve learned how to reach
and how to turn.

Now we work the side of the body
that delivers force.

Not by trying harder,
but by using the whole frame.

Here is Mac.


[4s MUSIC GAP — VANCE → MAC]


MAC (INTRO)

Mac here.
Session Four.

Today we focus on the rear hand.
The cross.
And we introduce the Rear Uppercut.

These punches don’t come from the arm.
They come from connection.

Feet.
Hips.
Shoulders.

We warm up first.


MAC (WARM-UP — CONNECTION)

Start in stance.
Hands up.

We’re waking up the chain.

Think of your body like a spring.
When it’s aligned, it releases smoothly.
When it’s twisted wrong, it jams.

We move together.

Set of four.

Turn. Set. One.
Turn. Set. Two.
Turn. Set. Three.
Turn. Set. Four.

Again.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Back to stance.


[2s MUSIC GAP — WARM-UP → CROSS CONTEXT]


MAC (CROSS — CONTEXT)

Rear hand.
The Cross.

This punch travels straight,
but it’s driven diagonally.

The back foot presses.
The hip turns.
The shoulder follows.

Imagine tightening a screw.
Slow at first.
Then it locks in place.

You don’t throw the arm.
You unwind the body.

We take it clean.


MAC (CROSS PRACTICE)

After me.
Set of four.

Cross. One.
Cross. Two.
Cross. Three.
Cross. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Shake it out.


[2s MUSIC GAP — CROSS → UPPERCUT CONTEXT]


MAC (REAR UPPERCUT — CONTEXT)

Now the Rear Uppercut.

This punch moves upward,
but it starts by settling down.

Think of an elevator.
It drops slightly,
then rises straight up the shaft.

Elbow stays close.
Palm faces you.
Power comes from the legs.

Short.
Direct.


MAC (REAR UPPERCUT PRACTICE)

After me.
Set of four.

Uppercut. One.
Uppercut. Two.
Uppercut. Three.
Uppercut. Four.

Reset.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

Last set.

One.
Two.
Three.
Four.

And stop. Hands down.


[2s MUSIC GAP — UPPERCUT → COMBINATION]


MAC (COMBINATION — CONTEXT)

Now we connect it.

Jab.
Cross.
Rear Uppercut.

Straight.
Straight.

MAC (COOLDOWN)

Take a breath.

Power comes from alignment,
not effort.

You stayed connected.

We’re done.


[4s MUSIC GAP — MAC → VANCE]


VANCE (OUTRO)

Session Four complete.

You’re learning how force really works.
Not by pushing,
but by linking things together.

Session Five takes this movement
and starts to protect it.

Rest well.
