Not Started 0 of 10 sessions
Laying Groundwork Sessions 1–4

You're learning to stand before you walk. Stance, guard, the basic punches. This is where discipline begins—not in speed or power, but in showing up and doing the work.

Building Rhythm Sessions 5–7

The punches are there. Now you learn to breathe between them. Rhythm isn't about going fast—it's about knowing when to go and when to wait. You're finding your tempo.

Holding Your Own Sessions 8–10

You can throw. You can move. Now you learn to stay in the pocket when it gets uncomfortable. This is where the work pays off—not in winning, but in not quitting.
